History Quiz - Learn and Test Your Knowledge

In what year did the Great Northern War, between Russia and Sweden, end?

  • 1726
  • 1727
  • 1724
  • 1721
The correct answer is 1721.
The Great Northern War ended in 1721, marking the end of Swedish Empire dominance in Northern Europe and the rise of Russia as a major power.

Which one of these tanks was designed and operated by the United Kingdom?

  • M4 Sherman
  • Tiger H1
  • T-34
  • Tog II
The correct answer is Tog II.
The Tog II was a British tank prototype developed during World War II. It was one of the heaviest tanks ever built by the UK.

What is the historical name of Sri Lanka?

  • Myanmar
  • Colombo
  • Badulla
  • Ceylon
The correct answer is Ceylon.
Sri Lanka was known as Ceylon until 1972, when it officially changed its name following the country becoming a republic.
