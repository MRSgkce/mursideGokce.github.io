Musicals & Theatres Quiz - Test Your Theatre Knowledge!

Who wrote the play 'Angels in America'?

  • Tom Stoppard
  • Matthew Lopez
  • Anthony Neilson
  • Tony Kusher
The correct answer is Tony Kusher.
Tony Kushner wrote the Pulitzer Prize-winning play "Angels in America," which deals with the AIDS epidemic and politics in the 1980s.

Which Shakespeare play inspired the musical 'West Side Story'?

  • Hamlet
  • Macbeth
  • Othello
  • Romeo & Juliet
The correct answer is Romeo & Juliet.
"West Side Story" is inspired by Shakespeare's "Romeo & Juliet" but is set in 1950s New York, focusing on the rivalry between two street gangs.

Who wrote the award-winning musical "In The Heights"?

  • Steven Sondheim
  • Francis Scott Key
  • John Phillips Sousa
  • Lin-Manuel Miranda
The correct answer is Lin-Manuel Miranda.
Lin-Manuel Miranda wrote the musical "In The Heights," which earned critical acclaim for its celebration of the Hispanic-American community and its fusion of music genres.
