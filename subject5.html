What do you call a baby bat?

  • Cub
  • Chick
  • Kid
  • Pup
The correct answer is Pup.
A baby bat is called a "pup". This term is used for the young of most bat species.

What is the name of a rabbit's abode?

  • Nest
  • Den
  • Dray
  • Burrow
The correct answer is Burrow.
Rabbits live in burrows, which are holes or tunnels dug in the ground for shelter.

What is Grumpy Cat's real name?

  • Minnie
  • Broccoli
  • Tardar Sauce
The correct answer is Tardar Sauce.
