How to Solve the Quiz

Follow these steps to get started with the quiz:

  • Step 1: Choose a quiz category from the homepage. You can select from Movies, Computers, History, Sports, or Animals.
  • Step 2: Read the question carefully before choosing your answer.
  • Step 3: Click on the answer option you think is correct. Once you click, your answer will be submitted automatically.
  • Step 4: After answering, you will be shown whether your answer was correct or incorrect, along with an explanation.
  • Step 5: Keep track of your score as you progress through the questions.
  • Step 6: At the end of the quiz, your final score will be displayed. You can choose to retry or move to a different category.

Good luck and have fun!
