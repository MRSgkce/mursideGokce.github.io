Film Quiz - Learn and Test Your Knowledge

Who plays the character of Po in the Kung Fu Panda movies?

  • Mirana Jonnes
  • McConahey Ramses
  • Jim Petersson
  • Jack Black
The correct answer is Jack Black.
Jack Black voices the main character Po, a lovable panda who dreams of becoming a kung fu master.

Which of these Movies was NOT released in 1996?

  • Independence Day
  • The Rock
  • Mission: Impossible
  • Gladiator
The correct answer is Gladiator.
"Gladiator" was released in 2000, not 1996.

In the movie Gremlins, after what time of day should you not feed Mogwai?

  • Evening
  • Morning
  • Afternoon
  • Midnight
The correct answer is Midnight.
In the film "Gremlins", feeding Mogwai after midnight leads to disastrous consequences!

In the Super Mario Bros. Movie (2023), who plays Toad?

  • Seth Rogan
  • Charlie Day
  • Jack Black
  • Keegan-Michael Key
The correct answer is Keegan-Michael Key.
Keegan-Michael Key voices the character Toad in the 2023 "Super Mario Bros." movie.

Who played Deputy Marshal Samuel Gerard in the 1993 film "The Fugitive"?

  • Harrison Ford
  • Harvey Keitel
  • Martin Landau
